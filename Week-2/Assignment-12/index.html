Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander, also credited mononymously as Anirudh, is an Indian music composer, music producer and singer br who works in the Indian film industry, primarily in Tamil film industry. He has also composed music for a few Hindi and Telugu films.He has become the go-to music director for all big-budget, star-studded movies. And he has also scored music for Thalapathy Vijay's upcoming movie Leo.
